Personnel reports


The system will, as personnel data inputted, generate personnel reports to facilitate personnel statistics and management.
Enter the main menu, and by default the [Department roll] will be shown. Select to show [Education composition analysis], [Personnel turnover report] (start and end dates shall be selected), or [Personnel card list]. After select a reports, its content will be shown below. The user can query as required, and can export reports into PDF, TXT, or CSV formats.

Department roll
The roll will be generated as department order, clearly showing departmental personnel details:


Education composition analysis
Shows education background summary of departmental personnel.


Personnel turnover report
Shows departmental personnel turnover report: new, in, out, quit, dismissed, normal departure. Start and end dates should be entered to query this report.


Personnel card list
Shows personnel card information. Card status is valid when being “1”, and is invalid when being “0”.
